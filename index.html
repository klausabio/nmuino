Apollo Flight Journal

I challenged myseft to redesign in 2 days the Apollo Flight Journal's identity and website, combining my passion for design and manned space exploration. The design was based in the 1960s flightplans, checklist and other documents NASA produced in the Apollo Era, and my goal was to improve the overall user experience, keeping it simple, intuitive and minimal.


The AFJ is an annotated transcript of the Apollo flights that follows the lead of the ALSJ (Apollo Lunar Sourface Journal) and is intended to be a resource for all those interested in the Apollo flights to the Moon, whether in a passing or scholarly capacity.
